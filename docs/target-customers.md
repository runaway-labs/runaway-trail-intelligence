# Target Customers

## Ideal first race org profile

- Ultra or trail race, ideally backyard ultra format
- 100–400 runners (big enough to matter, small enough to deliver)
- Independent race director or small race org (not a major series)
- Established event with website, contact form, social presence
- Race date in summer/fall 2026 (3–6 month runway)
- Geographic location: doesn't matter — best fit wins

## Why backyard ultras specifically

- Loop format means course knowledge is repeatable across all athletes (one course model serves the whole race)
- Athletes have natural downtime between loops where audio coaching fits without interfering
- Field sizes are typically small (40–150 runners) — manageable for a POC
- Backyard RD community is tight-knit; one good reference unlocks others
- The format actively rewards mental and pacing strategy, which is exactly what AI coaching can enhance
